About Dana's Time Out


Dana’s Time Out, established in 2004, was Geneva’s first themed Restaurant & Sports Bar.

We are a full service restaurant located at 258 Hamilton Street (Routes 5 & 20) directly across

from the Hobart & William Smith Colleges Boswell Field.


We offer menu items such as Steak, Pasta & Seafood, Burgers, Wings & Wraps.

We serve our full menu Tuesday-Thursday until 9pm and Friday-Saterday until Midnight.

We have catered to Many events onsite and offsite at the Colleges, Pre-Game Dinners, Baby Showers,

Office Parties, Bridal Showers, Birthday Parties,Box Lunches and subs for bus rides and parties

following the local sporting events.


The atmosphere screams support for New York’s sports teams including the NFL, NHL, NBA and MLB.

MLB. There are several large screen televisions placed throughout the restaurant. We sponsor

parties for all major sporting events such as the Super Bowl and the World Series as well as a

semi-annual Golf Tournaments and an annual Bowling Tournament. Occasionally, we have Live

Music and/or DJ’s. The atmosphere is upbeat and casual.
